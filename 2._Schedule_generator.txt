====== 2. Schedule generator ======
Created Thursday 22 April 2021

=== Problem statement ===
Given:
1. Knowledge graph
2. A list of available time slot(s). By default - the whole day is represented.
3. A list of goals - there may be none. For example: the subject is a hobby.

Generate a schedule.
